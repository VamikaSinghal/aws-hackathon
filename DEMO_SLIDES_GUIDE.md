## 🏆 HOW TO WIN THE ROOM

- **Open strong:** The personal trainer question hooks them
- **Show something real:** Live demo beats slides every time
- **Tell a complete story:** Problem → Solution → Vision → Impact
- **End with clarity:** "It doesn't recommend. It acts." (Repeat this)
- **Leave them wanting more:** Don't over-explain, let them ask questions

**They should leave thinking:** 
*"This is the future of health AI, and it exists today."*

---

## ❌ WHAT NOT TO DO

- ❌ Don't spend time explaining basic AI concepts
- ❌ Don't read from slides (have them as reminders only)
- ❌ Don't apologize if demo has minor glitches ("It's working!")
- ❌ Don't oversell features that aren't in the demo
- ❌ Don't use jargon without explaining it
- ❌ Don't end with questions—end with your message
