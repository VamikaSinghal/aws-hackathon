"Look at today's situation. User slept only 5.2 hours. Their HRV is critically low—28ms. Recovery is just 23%. But they have a HIIT workout scheduled at 7am.

**This is dangerous. The system needs to intervene.**"

### Demo Point 2: Multi-Agent Debate (1:25-2:00)

**[Click through agents - let messages flow]**

"Watch what happens. Four AI agents step in:

**The Data Collector** syncs from all sources—Apple Health, Oura Ring, Calendar, Gmail. It sees the danger.

**The Planner** generates 3 options: Do the HIIT anyway, do a light walk instead, or rest completely.

**The Critic** evaluates each against safety thresholds. It sees HRV is 28ms, but the minimum safe threshold is 40ms. It REJECTS the HIIT workout. Too risky. It APPROVES the light walk—gentle movement that aids recovery.

**The Optimizer** builds the final action plan: Cancel the workout, add a 20-minute walk at 7:30am, send a magnesium reminder, block screens after 10:30pm, and ensure 8 hours of sleep.

**All of this happens in 2.3 seconds. Autonomously. Without human input.**

This isn't a suggestion. This is an executed plan."

2. **Pause after key statements** - Let impact sink in
3. **Make eye contact** - Connect with audience, not screen
4. **Use hand gestures** - Point to specific elements in demo
5. **Vary your pace** - Slow down for important points, speed up for lists
6. **Show genuine excitement** - This is health AI that WORKS
7. **Let the demo speak** - The autonomous agents are the story
